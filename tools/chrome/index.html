windfoil — Chrome <canvas> vs @napi-rs/canvas

Renders the validate.js shape set with Chrome's Canvas 2D and diffs it against the napi-rs (Skia) coverage captured by deno task validate, pixel-for-pixel. "chrome vs napi" is the direct disagreement between the two Skia builds; "chrome vs box" mirrors validate's "skia vs box" column so you can cross-check the terminal. Numbers and a per-shape breakdown are also logged to the console.
